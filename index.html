(b) 2024 DJI Robomaster Intelligent UAV Championship for Planning and Control

Our IM-NMPO framework was successfully implemented in the 2024 DJI Robomaster Intelligent UAV Championship for Planning and Control of Quadrotors, where it completed the challenging racing courses with speeds up to 23.3 m/s, ranking first and finishing in less than one-third of the time taken by the second-place.

(c) 2024 DJI Robomaster Intelligent UAV Championship for Autonomous Perception and Racing

IM-NMPO can rapidly replan new time-optimal trajectories based on real-time visual detection results, completing the course in 72 seconds with a maximum velocity of 10.9 m/s, outperforming the best record of 74 s.
